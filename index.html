03.

UNM Club Memo

An internal memo to club members proposing a campus parking solution.

Lobo Parking
Advocating for better parking and transport
Date
February 5th, 2026
To
All Lobo Parking Members
From
Grace Tomallo, Lead Officer
Subject
Proposal for Uber X subscription included in UNM student tuition.

The purpose of this memo is to address the parking shortage we have here at UNM as well as discuss the contents of a meeting to discuss possible solutions to this problem.

Many students struggle on a daily basis to find available parking spaces, often arriving late or even missing class due to our overcrowded lots. This problem is not only frustrating but affects students’ academic performance as well. Despite permit costs, parking availability doesn’t keep up with the number of students who have cars on campus.

One potential solution our club would like to explore is partnering with Uber X or ride-share services and including the cost in tuition. This program would reduce the number of cars on campus, ease congestion, and give students reliable transportation.

There will be a meeting held on Friday the 6th at 4:00 pm at Dane Smith Hall to discuss the issue and solutions further.

If you have any questions before the meeting, feel free to contact me at Gtomallo@unm.edu.
